Welcome to Any.Prog.

Any.Prog is based upon the idea that Computer Science is free for all and programming is such an art that must be taught to everyone, irresepctive of academic or language barriers. As the Programing is an art of brain and thought process, we always think in our mother-tongue so I thought of making a project that can allow people to learn the basics of programming in their own mothertongue.
